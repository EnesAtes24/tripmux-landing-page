Tripmux

We are currently building Tripmux.

Tripmux will help travelers compare flights, trains, and buses in one place and find the cheapest and most convenient routes between cities.

Our goal is to show smart route combinations (for example: flight + train or flight + bus) and then redirect users to trusted partners to complete their bookings.
