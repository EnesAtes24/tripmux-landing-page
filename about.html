About Tripmux

Tripmux is a travel search platform focused on helping travelers find cheaper routes by combining different transportation options.

Instead of only showing direct flights, Tripmux explores alternative nearby cities and future combinations of flights, trains, and buses to reduce the total cost of travel.

Our goal is simple: not the fastest route, but the cheapest total journey.

Tripmux is currently in its early stage (MVP) and already provides live flight price comparisons. We are actively expanding to include rail and bus connections across Europe and beyond.

Our mission is to make smarter travel accessible to everyone by using price optimization instead of traditional route search logic.
